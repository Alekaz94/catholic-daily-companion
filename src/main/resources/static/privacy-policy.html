Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

Thank you for using Catholic Daily Companion ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy matters deeply to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

By using the app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

• Full name
• Email address
• Google account information (if signing in via Google)
• IP address (collected automatically by the backend)
• Journal entries you create
• Rosary logs, prayer history, and streaks
• Audit logs for security and debugging

What We Do NOT Collect

• We do not collect or store your Google password.
• We do not receive sensitive Google data (contacts, files, etc.)

How We Use Your Information

• To authenticate you securely through Google
• To display your journal and rosary history
• To maintain your rosary streak system
• To improve app performance (e.g., caching saints)
• To comply with legal requirements

Ads & Analytics

We use Google Mobile Ads. Only anonymized usage data is collected for ad performance. We do not share your personal data with advertisers.

Data Security

We use HTTPS, secure token-based authentication, and industry-standard measures. Since no password is ever stored by us, that sensitive data never enters our system.
