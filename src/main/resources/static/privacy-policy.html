Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

Thank you for using Catholic Daily Companion (“we,” “our,” or “us”). Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Age Restriction:

This app is intended for users 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full name and email address – to create your account and allow secure authentication.
  • Google account information (if signing in via Google) – only your name and email. No passwords are collected.
  • IP address – automatically collected for security, abuse prevention, and analytics.
  • Journal entries – to store and display your personal reflections.
  • Rosary logs – your rosary history, streaks, and completion status.
  • Feedback emails – viewed only by administrators to respond to user inquiries or improve the app.
  • Audit logs – for security, debugging, and detecting unauthorized activity.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect Google passwords or other sensitive Google data (e.g., contacts, files).
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Secure authentication via Google.
  • Displaying your journal entries and rosary history.
  • Maintaining rosary streaks and progress.
  • Responding to feedback and improving app functionality.
  • Analyzing app performance and usage trends (anonymized).
  • Complying with legal obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Authentication (OAuth2) – for secure sign-in. Only your name and email are received.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) – displays ads. Only anonymized usage and device data may be collected.
  • Railway Hosting – backend and database hosting. Your journal entries, rosary logs, and account records are stored securely.
  • PostgreSQL Database – stores journal entries, rosary data, logs, and other content you create.
  • Expo / React Native Services – may collect anonymized diagnostics (e.g., app crashes, device type).

5. Data Security

We implement HTTPS, secure token-based authentication, and industry-standard practices to protect your data. Feedback emails are accessible only by app administrators. Sensitive data such as passwords are never stored.

6. Your Rights

You may update, delete, or request deletion of your data by contacting: alexandros.kazalis@gmail.com. You can also delete your account directly in the app, which will remove your journal entries, rosary logs, and account information. Currently, there is no email-based method to view your stored data; however, you can export a copy of your data directly from the app using the “Export JSON” or "Export ZIP" buttons in Settings. Requests for deletion via email will be addressed promptly, typically within 30 days.

7. GDPR Rights

EU users can access, correct, delete, or export their data. Most actions can be performed directly in the app.

8. Data Retention

Your data is stored until you delete your account. Journal entries and rosary logs can be deleted at any time.

9. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. Parental supervision is recommended for young users.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, users will be notified in the app or via email.
